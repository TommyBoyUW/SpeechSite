Activities for Speech Sound Disorder / Articulation Therapy

Use a toy mailbox or empty box with a lid to “mail” your speech sound cards

Write target sounds, words, and/or sentences on hearts and glue to a piece of paper

Valentine’s Day Speech Sound Targets:

  • /k/ - candy, card, cupcake, chocolate, cookie
  • /v/ - valentine, envelope
  • /l/ - love, like, lollipop, valentine, lace, mail, letter, glue, chocolate, mailbox, envelope
  • /r/ - red, rose, heart, card, friend, paper, scissors
  • /s/ - send, stamp, scissors, mailbox
